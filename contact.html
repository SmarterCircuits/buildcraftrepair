Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

🛠️

Do you take custom orders?

Yes! We love custom commissions. Get in touch with your dimensions, wood preference, and finish and we'll put together a quote.

🚚

Do you ship?

Smaller items ship via Etsy and Amazon. For large furniture pieces we can arrange freight shipping — contact us for a quote based on your location.

🪑

Can you restore my furniture?

We occasionally take on restoration commissions. Send us photos and a description of the piece and we'll let you know if it's something we can help with.

⏳

How long do custom orders take?

Lead times vary by project. Simple pieces can be ready in 2–4 weeks; complex furniture may take 6–10 weeks. We'll give you a realistic timeline upfront.

🌲

What wood species do you use?

We work with white oak, black walnut, hard maple, cherry, and pine most frequently. Reclaimed and specialty timber available on request.

📹

Can my piece be on YouTube?

We document most of our builds! If you'd prefer your commission stays private, just let us know — we're happy to keep it off-camera.
