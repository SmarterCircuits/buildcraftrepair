Our Story

Build Craft Repair was born out of a simple desire: to make things that last. In a world of flat-pack furniture and fast fashion, we believe in solid hardwoods, traditional joinery, and the kind of craftsmanship that gets passed down through generations.

What started as a personal workshop project quickly grew into a community. We began filming our builds and restorations, sharing every step — the sawdust, the mistakes, and the moments when a piece finally comes together. Our YouTube channel is a behind-the- scenes look at every item in this catalogue.

Today we create original handcrafted pieces as well as restoring antique furniture that deserves a second life. Everything we make is available to purchase, either directly through this site, on Etsy, or on Amazon Handmade.

What We Stand For

Everything we make is guided by these principles.

🪵

Quality Materials

We use solid hardwoods and responsibly sourced timber — no MDF, no shortcuts. If it's not good enough for us, it's not good enough for you.

🔨

Traditional Craft

Dovetails, mortise-and-tenon, hand-planing — we use the joinery techniques that have stood the test of time for good reason.

♻️

Sustainability

Restoring antique furniture keeps it out of landfill. Using reclaimed wood gives new purpose to old growth timber. We are committed to making sustainable choices.

📹

Transparency

Every build is documented on YouTube. We show the whole process — including the bits that go wrong — so you know exactly what goes into every piece.

🤝

Community

We love helping people discover woodworking and furniture restoration. Ask us anything on YouTube or reach out directly — we're always happy to chat craft.

⭐

Built to Last

We don't make disposable furniture. Every piece is built to be handed down, repaired, and used for decades. That's the Build Craft Repair guarantee.
